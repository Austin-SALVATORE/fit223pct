# App icon — Round 1 concept sheet

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Asset | App icon / favicon replacement |
| Lineage | Brand iconography — independent of the exercise illustration system |
| Palette | Accent `#D9A048` warm amber, optional cream tint `#F2E8D8`, ground `#191512` near-black |
| Round | 1 — concept sheet, three directions × two variants |
| Status | `planned` — owner picks a direction from this sheet |

> New prompt lineage: no goblet-squat anchor, no exercise style block.
> Only the palette values carry over from the illustration system.
> Brand theme: "Fit 2 23%" — the journey toward a 23% body-fat target.
> The numerals "23" and "%" are deliberate brand marks and are allowed;
> generic letters and words are banned.

## Prompt

```text
Create one app-icon concept sheet for a premium fitness app, laid out as a
clean design presentation on a single flat neutral mid-gray background
(#6E6A66). No text, no captions, no labels, no annotations anywhere on the
sheet.

THE GRID: exactly six large app-icon tiles arranged in three columns and two
rows, evenly spaced with generous gray gutters. Every tile is an identical
rounded square (corner radius about 22% of the tile width) filled with flat
solid near-black #191512. The icon mark sits centered inside its tile and
occupies at most 60% of the tile width — generous empty near-black margin on
all sides.

BOTTOM STRIP: below the grid, one horizontal row repeating all six icon
marks in the same left-to-right order, each rendered inside a tiny rounded
square only 1/12 the size of the large tiles, spaced evenly. This simulates
favicon size: each tiny mark must stay recognizable, not a smudge.

STYLE — IDENTICAL FOR ALL SIX ICONS:
Flat vector iconography. Solid flat shapes only: no gradients, no shading,
no 3D bevels, no shadows, no glows, no outlines around the tiles, no
texture. Exactly two ink colors are available on the near-black ground:
warm amber #D9A048 (primary) and soft cream #F2E8D8 (optional secondary
accent, used sparingly or not at all). Crisp geometric construction,
confident thick strokes, premium and modern like a top-tier training app.

COLUMN 1 — PERCENT-DUMBBELL FUSION (two variants, top and bottom):
A percent sign "%" redrawn so it is simultaneously a dumbbell: its two
circles are drawn as weight plates — thick amber rings or solid amber discs
with a small round center hole — and its diagonal stroke is the straight
bar connecting them, drawn as one clean thick amber bar at roughly 45
degrees. The whole glyph must still read instantly as a percent sign.
Top tile: both plates as bold open rings, bar passing between them.
Bottom tile: both plates as solid discs with punched center holes, with two
short cream collar ticks where the bar meets each plate.

COLUMN 2 — GOAL GAUGE (two variants, top and bottom):
A circular progress gauge meaning "almost at the goal": a ring-shaped track
with a clearly visible small notch or tick marker on the ring, and a thick
amber progress arc sweeping clockwise from the top and stopping just short
of that notch. No numerals in this column — the notch is the goal.
Top tile: a single bold open amber ring whose gap IS the remaining
distance, with a small cream tick marking the goal position just past the
arc's end.
Bottom tile: a thin cream full-circle track underneath, with the thick
amber progress arc on top of it and a short amber notch crossing the track
at the goal position; small round amber dot at the arc's leading end.

COLUMN 3 — "23" NUMERAL MONOGRAM (two variants, top and bottom):
The number "23" set large in an elegant high-contrast display serif
typeface (in the spirit of Fraunces: soft wedge serifs, generous curves,
strong thick-thin contrast), solid amber, tightly kerned so the two digits
almost touch, confident like a jersey number, perfectly centered.
Top tile: "23" alone, as large as the safe zone allows.
Bottom tile: the same "23" slightly smaller with a small cream percent sign
"%" tucked in as a superscript at the upper right of the 3.

STRICTLY EXCLUDE: any letters or words, any numerals other than the "23"
and "%" specified above, human figures or body parts, gradients, shadows,
glows, outlines, 3D effects, photographic texture, captions, labels,
watermarks, grid lines, arrows, more than the two ink colors on the
near-black tiles.
```

## Gate (advisory — owner's eyeball is the acceptance gate)

- [ ] All six tiles identical rounded-square #191512, mark ≤ ~60% of tile
- [ ] Flat shapes only — no gradient/shadow/glow anywhere
- [ ] Max two ink colors (amber + optional cream)
- [ ] Column A reads as BOTH "%" and dumbbell
- [ ] Column B notch position reads as a goal marker; no numerals
- [ ] Column C serif has Fraunces-like contrast; digits legible
- [ ] Bottom 16px-scale strip: each mark still recognizable
- [ ] No stray text/labels on the sheet
