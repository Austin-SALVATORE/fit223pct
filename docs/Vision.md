# Vision

Fit 2 23% is a premium personal training operating system for one user: Austin.
The name is the goal: fit, down to 23% body fat.
It is not a workout tracker. It is the calm, intelligent layer between Austin
and his training — it already knows what today looks like.

## North star

> Would Austin genuinely enjoy opening this every morning before training?

## What it does

- Answers "What do I do today?" instantly — Today is always the primary screen.
- Runs the workout: current exercise, previous performance, editable load/reps,
  RIR, rest timer, technique cues, substitutions. Never interrupts flow.
- Remembers everything locally. Never loses history. Works offline.
- Teaches one concept at the right moment (hip hinge on RDL day, bracing on squat day).
- Quietly detects stagnation and suggests progression — invisible intelligence, no chatbot.
- Celebrates discipline, never punishes missed sessions. Recovery is progress.

## What it is not

- Not a SaaS dashboard. Not gamified. Not neon. Not military coaching.
- No feature exists because "fitness apps have it." Every feature earns its place.

## User context

- Austin, 180 cm, 92–93 kg, goal ~23% body fat while preserving muscle.
- Plays tennis and swims; uses Apple Fitness+.
- Phase 1 (21 Jul – 9 Aug): home gym — barbell 0–30 kg, dumbbells (1×20 kg or 2×10 kg),
  adjustable bench, bands. Phase 2 (10 Aug →): Fitness Park gym.

## Success metrics

Primary: consistency, waist trend, strength trend.
Secondary: scale weight, session completion.
